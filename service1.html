Services

Sea Freight

MLC specializes in providing expert international oceanfreight forwarding services to companies large and small. We are an integral part of our clients export departments, offering custom tailored ocean/sea freight logistics solutions for a wide variety of cargo.

We offer all types of containerized shipping from Booking, Stowage, Making Documents, Clearing Customs, Monitoring Packing, Cargo and Loading Inspections; Also Keep the balance among ship, cargo and port. Offering FCL and LCL shipment service. Container Carring, Leasing, Stacking, and Applying Inspecting business. Cargo Export Agency, including Customs Clearance, Quarantine, Commodity Inspection, Health Inspection. Offering the Customs Clearance at destination port, including Door-to-Door Service.

we will provide Updating the latest freight rate, Tracking cargo information, Issuing shipping documents,Calculating and Charging fee. Helping clients design the best and efficient logistics transpotation solutions.
Our staff is known for it's extensive experience in handling all facets of ocean cargo logistics operations. MLC is a leading project cargo specialist, often handling extremely difficult and large scale freight to remote destinations.

We are therefore able to offer our customers a comprehensive range of ocean freight services :

  • Export and Import Services
  • LCL (Less than a container load)
  • FCL (Full container load)
  • Full Documentation
  • Port-to-Port/Door-to-door
  • Hazardous Cargo
  • Customs Clearance and Compliance
